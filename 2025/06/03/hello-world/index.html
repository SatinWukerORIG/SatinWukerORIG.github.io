WHY TOC?

The reason I started reading the theories of computation is, I was once overcome by the “hardest problem in computer science”, the P vs. NP problem. This asks whether every problem whose solution can be quickly verified (NP) can be quickly solved (P). In other words, it is asking: easy cheking == easy solving? We will talk about it later in Complexity Theory.
